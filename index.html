10th Anniversary

In last 10 years had produced 4200 software engineers to the nation....!

ABCD was started on the 10th January of 2006 in Colombo, opening doors for the younger generation to materialize their dream of becoming a software engineering / quality assurance Engineer through providing recognized qualifications in IT and Software Engineering. At the start ABCD marched forward with a highly dedicated team of ten individuals whose hard work resulted in opening the second branch in Anuradhapure. Within this short period of time, ABCD has achieved a leading position among the providers of Software Engineering degrees in Sri Lanka.

The uniqueness of realizing the demanded programmers and ABCD’s infinite flexibilities to explore new ways and best products could mark the journey a fast moving result oriented one. This achievement in short has made our degree in software engineering highly desired and sought after out of all other IT and Computer Science degrees in Sri Lanka. The Committed individuals of our team and student centered values have brought ABCD from 30 to 700 students following our various courses in IT and Software Engineering. Our records have indicated that we had produced more than 4200 valuable software engineering professionals to the nation, and as a Sri Lankan IT education Institute we are proud to say that, many students of our first batch were recruited by most prestigious software companies in Sri Lanka placing us above all other IT course providers from the very beginning of our journey as an institute for higher education in software engineering and IT/Computer Science.
